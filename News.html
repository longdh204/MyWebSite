Hanoi, October 11, 2024 - Today, the city of Hanoi inaugurated the 2024 Technology and Innovation Fair, a major event that has attracted hundreds of businesses and startups from around the world. The fair, which runs from October 11 to 13, showcases cutting-edge products and technological solutions across various industries. The opening ceremony was attended by prominent figures in the tech industry, government officials, and international delegates. The event features a series of activities, including workshops, exhibitions, and networking sessions aimed at fostering collaboration and innovation. One of the highlights of the fair is the “Future Tech” exhibition, where companies present their latest advancements in artificial intelligence, robotics, and sustainable technologies. Attendees have the opportunity to experience interactive demonstrations and learn about the potential impact of these innovations on everyday life. In addition to the exhibitions, the fair also hosts several panel discussions and keynote speeches from industry leaders. Topics range from the future of smart cities to the role of technology in addressing global challenges such as climate change and healthcare. The fair provides a platform for startups to pitch their ideas to potential investors and partners. The “Startup Pitch” competition, a key event of the fair, offers young entrepreneurs the chance to showcase their projects and receive valuable feedback from a panel of judges. Overall, the 2024 Technology and Innovation Fair in Hanoi is a celebration of creativity and progress, highlighting the importance of technological advancements in shaping a better future.
